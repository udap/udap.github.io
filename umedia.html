umx.art
An NFT Trading Platform Built on Blockchain Technology
umx assigns an irreplaceable and unforgeable code to each artwork using blockchain technology and realizes an artwork’s completely transparent release on the umx platform; the smart contract’s specific royalty setting realizes an artist’s lifelong royalty income.
The core of our business mode is on the primary and secondary markets. After setting the parameters of an NFT’s price, releasing amount, releasing time, and royalty rate through the blockchain smart contract offered by umx, artists can then release artworks on our primary market, which supports both selling at a fixed price and bidding. Consumers can put the artworks received from the primary market on the secondary market for recycling and changing hands, benefiting from their investments as the artworks appreciate in value.
