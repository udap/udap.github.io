UDAP is a blockchain middleware that provides common services for non-fungible asset lifecycle management, search, and trading. It creates and manages bidirectional bindings between real world assets and their crypto representations, provides an abstraction layer for accessing underlying blockchain infrastructure, and helps Internet applications such as MLS.com easily transform themselves into token-based business.

  • Protocol

    A Universal Decentralized Asset Protocol as the backbone of the Internet of Assets

  • Service

    An Asset Blockchain as a Service (ABaaS) for private & consortium asset blockchain deployment

  • Chain

    A Decentralized Middleware for Asset Lifecycle Management

  • Marketplace

    A Global C2C Marketplace for asset trading and exchange without intermeditary

  • Wallet

    A Universal Asset Wallet that tracks and manages tokens and assets

  • Enabler

    A Technology Enabler for business to transform from centralized internet model to a token-based economy
